Resume Mastery Guide

Types of Resumes

Chronological

Lists your work history in reverse chronological order. Best for those with steady employment history.

Functional

Focuses on skills rather than work history. Ideal for career changers or those with employment gaps.

Combination

Mixes chronological and functional formats. Highlights skills while providing work history.
