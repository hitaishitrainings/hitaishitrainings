Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2025

At Hitaishi Trainings, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details provided through forms.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device info, and usage patterns via cookies or analytics tools.
  • Payment Information: If you enroll in paid courses, your payment details are processed securely through trusted gateways.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our training services and website.
  • To personalize your learning experience and recommend suitable courses.
  • To send updates, newsletters, or promotional materials (only with your consent).
  • To improve our website based on user behavior and feedback.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted partners or third-party service providers to help operate our business (e.g., payment processors, email services), only under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You may unsubscribe from promotional emails anytime using the link provided.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance user experience. You can choose to disable cookies via your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
