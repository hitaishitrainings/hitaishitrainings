Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the job placement process typically take?

The timeline varies based on your current skill level, experience, and the job market. On average, our students secure positions within 3-5 months of starting our program. We work at your pace to ensure you're fully prepared before applying for jobs.

Do you guarantee job placement?

While we can't guarantee employment (as the final hiring decision rests with employers), we do guarantee our support throughout your job search. Our 94% placement rate speaks to our effectiveness. We provide all the tools, training, and connections you need to succeed.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have strong placement networks in technology (software development, data science, cybersecurity), business (marketing, finance, HR), healthcare, and creative fields (design, content writing). Our career coaches have industry-specific expertise to guide you in your chosen field.

Is there ongoing support after I get a job?

Absolutely! We provide 6 months of post-placement support including salary negotiation advice, onboarding guidance, and career progression planning. Many of our alumni continue to benefit from our network and resources throughout their careers.
